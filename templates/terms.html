crimbot - An experiment by crimeraaa

Welcome to crimbot! To start chatting with the bot, please familiarize yourself with some information:

  • The bot may generate content that is offensive. Use at your own risk.
  • Do not give the bot any personally identifiable information, as it models its responses after the messages it receives.
  • Remember to not take anything the bot says seriously. It can make stuff up, contradict itself or claim it is a real person.
  • We store cookies in your browser to keep track of which messages belong to which conversation. The message data you submit in itself does not contain any personally identifiable information.
  • The experiment lasts until October 5th, 2023. After that date, the site will be gone. Enjoy it while you still can.
